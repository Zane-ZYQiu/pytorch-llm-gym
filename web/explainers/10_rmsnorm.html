Interactive · RMSNorm vs LayerNorm

LayerNorm re-centers (subtract mean) and re-scales. RMSNorm only divides by RMS = √(mean(x²)+ε) — no mean subtraction, no β. Note RMSNorm keeps the mean offset.

RMSNorm: x / RMS  
LayerNorm: (x−μ)/σ  

Same input, γ=1. LayerNorm output is always centered at mean ≈ 0; RMSNorm keeps the original sign/offset of the mean (it skips re-centering).
